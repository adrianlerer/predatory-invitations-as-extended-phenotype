## Critical Feature: Accepts PPTs and Videos

### "Video Articles and PowerPoint Presentations (PPTs)"

**This is absurd for academic publishing.**

**Why legitimate journals DON'T accept PPTs:**
1. **Not peer-reviewable** - slides lack methodological detail
2. **Not archival** - format changes make long-term access difficult
3. **Not citable** - academic standards require textual papers
4. **Not scholarly** - PPTs are presentation aids, not primary literature

**Why PSO DOES accept them:**
- **Lowers barrier** - more submissions
- **No review needed** - can't peer-review a PPT anyway
- **Faster processing** - less text to pretend to review
