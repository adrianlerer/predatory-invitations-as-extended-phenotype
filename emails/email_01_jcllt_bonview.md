## Lessons for Authors

### Positive Indicators (Present in This Case)

✅ **Editor has real identity**
- LinkedIn profile with activity
- Institutional affiliation visible
- Published work in the field
- Named in invitation with credentials

✅ **Publisher has institutional markers**
- OASPA membership
- Multiple journals (not single-purpose shell)
- Physical address provided
- Transparent policies

✅ **Professional infrastructure**
- OJS or equivalent platform
- Not email submission
- Realistic timelines
- Standard peer review described

✅ **Field alignment**
- Journal scope matches paper topic
- Editorial board expertise relevant
- No catch-all keywords

✅ **Transparent communication**
- No aggressive follow-ups
- Policies stated clearly
- Responsive to questions
- No hidden fees revealed later

### Red Flags (Absent in This Case)

❌ Unverifiable editor identity  
❌ Gmail or generic email domains  
❌ Email attachment submission  
❌ Disciplinary mismatch  
❌ Phantom follow-ups  
❌ Template errors  
❌ Unrealistic review timelines (days)  
❌ Price before review
