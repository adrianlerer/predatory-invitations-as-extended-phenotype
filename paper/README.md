## Writing Principles

### Academic Rigor

- **Claims backed by evidence:** Every assertion tied to corpus or literature
- **Falsifiability:** PSO framework includes testable predictions
- **Alternative explanations:** Address principal-agent, moral hazard, regulatory capture
- **Limitations acknowledged:** Qualitative approach, small corpus, single-author observation

### Conceptual Clarity

- **PSO defined operationally:** 5-criterion identification matrix
- **EPT applied precisely:** Not metaphorical, but mechanistic
- **Terminology consistent:** Replicator, phenotype, selection, niche used rigorously

### Accessibility

- **Bridge disciplines:** Evolutionary biology + institutional economics + legal theory
- **Examples concrete:** Each theoretical point illustrated with corpus case
- **Jargon minimized:** Technical terms explained when introduced
